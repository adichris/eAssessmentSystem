Continuous Electronic Assessment System

Continuous Electronic Assessment System assesses student academic in both Midsem and Theory, in the areas of multichoice and theory questions, and giving the lecture total control of the scripts thus the script markings, records, publishing them.

Electronic Assessment can take place directly at lecture hall (class room), or any place on any device (having a web browser), and even through their devices beyond the campus.

Modern day Continuous Assessment System has become physically easier as new technologies have enabled more diverse, universal and widespread continuous assessment practices that include e-learning portals and online MultiChoice quizzes. Moreover, the boundaries of what constitutes academics is becoming increasingly porous, especially as digital devices and technologies facilitate much of our academic content and learning both remotely and on institutional campus.

Continuous Assessment Inputs

All the data will be inputted into the system electronically. The data includes lectures questions and marking scheme, student answers, and publishing of result will be done through the computer. Moreover, work becomes very easy because student works will mark automatically, and there will no need to keep data on papers. The scope of the project is to developed web application, that is the project is developed as a responsive web application, and it will work for every academic institution.

Multiple Choice Questions

Multiple Choice Questions are questions type where the student has to choose or select an option or a choice from the available choices. In our Continuous eAssessment System every multiple-choice question can have any number of choices, where students are to select one of them in every question. When lectures are setting up questions, they are required to indicate a correct option or choice from the rest of the choices for a particular question. Multichoice assessment questions are marked automatically by the system based on the correct option or choices indicated by the lecture and the option or choice the student choice.


Theory Assessment

In this continuous assessment system, theory assessment is designed in the likeness to the physical theory quizzes, and midsem questions, where students are expected to provide in writing solutions to the questions. The difference ours is electronically based instead of on paper quiz. The student is expected to type in solutions to respectively questions on the page.
