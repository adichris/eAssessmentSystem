CONTINUOUS ASSESSMENT SYSTEM

The purpose of developing this continuous assessment system is to computerized the tradition way of taking continuous assessment. Another purpose for developing this web application is to assess student electronical. Some of the purpose of this continuous assessment:

  • Enhancing the student’s learning.
    Continuous assessment of the knowledge that the student acquires during the course of study is very important. This also ensures that the students are in constant touch with the curriculum and all that it has to offer.
  • Improving the faculty’s teaching skills.
    The pressure of continuous assessment can ensure optimum performance of the teachers as well. This is achieved by reducing the workload on the lecture. Let’s say that a lecture wants to conduct quizzes for hundred student, this system reduces the time that the lectures take to correct tests and exercises, and help mark and distribute the result to student easily. Giving the lecture time and space to focus on tuition.
